About Us

  • We are a family run kennel located less than an hour outside of Regina...on the beautiful prairies of Saskatchewan, Canada. We have a large rural property where our Berners have plenty of room to run, play, and explore.

  • We are a mother daughter team, Karla Hanson and Brooke Zanzen and we live here with our family of 3 Bernese Mountain Dogs... Gypsy, Stella, and Bear.

  • All of our Bernese Mountain Dogs are raised in our home. They lounge on our furniture and sleep indoors. They love to hangout watching tv or movies, and just want to be by the familyâ€™s side. They also enjoy endless time outdoors. They love playing in the yard any season..... and love a good snowy day! We keep a small number of dogs so that they can receive the love and attention that they each deserve. They are greatly loved and cared for.

  • We have carefully selected our breeding dogs for excellent health and longevity. They come from a very reputable breeder whose dogs have won many championships here in Canada. Our breeding dogs are CKC registered and have passed OFA testing for heart, hips, elbows, eyes, and VonWillibrands desease. We focus on raising well socialized happy puppies in a family atmosphere. Our puppies are raised in the house where they interact with family friends and other children, cats and dogs through their first 8-9 weeks of life. Our puppies are CKC registered, wormed, vet checked, microchipped, and have their first shots. They are sold with a nonbreeding contract, and a written two year health guarantee.
